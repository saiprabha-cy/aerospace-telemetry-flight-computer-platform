# Ground Station

## Purpose

The Ground Station receives telemetry packets and displays mission status.

Engineers use the Ground Station to monitor spacecraft health during flight.

---

## Responsibilities

- Receive telemetry packets
- Display mission information
- Generate mission reports
- Count healthy and warning packets

---

## Output

mission_report.txt
